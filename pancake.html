Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

1½ cups all-purpose flour
3½ teaspoons baking powder
1 tablespoon granulated sugar
¼ teaspoon salt
1¼ cups milk
3 tablespoons butter, melted
1 egg


Directions

1. Sift the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together in a large bowl.
2. Add the milk, melted butter, and egg; mix until smooth.
3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat
4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately ¼ cup for each pancake; cook until bubbles form,about 2 to 3 minutes
5. Flip and cook until browned on the other side
6. Repeat with remaining batter
7. Enjoy!!
