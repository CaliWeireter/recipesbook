Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

1 cups salted butter, softened
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup light brown sugar, packed
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 large eggs
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
½ tsp baking powder
1 tsp sea salt
2 cups chocolate chips


Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F and line a baking pan with parchment paper
2. In a bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, salt, and baking powder
3. Cream together the butter and sugars until combined
4. Beat in the eggs and vanilla until fluffy
5. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined
6. Add the chocolate chips and mix well
7. Roll out the dough into balls however large you want the cookies to be
8. Place on the baking pan and bake fo 8-10 minutes or until golden brown on the edges
9. Let cool for a few minutes after taking out of oven
9. Enjoy!!
